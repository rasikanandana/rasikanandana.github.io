About Me

PhD candidate at Victoria University of Wellington, focusing on Earthquake Early Warning (EEW) systems, Mechatronics, and IoT. Former Lecturer in Mechanical Engineering at the Open University of Sri Lanka. Experienced in automation, control theory, and data science.

Research

My research focuses on the development and evaluation of Earthquake Early Warning (EEW) systems using low-cost MEMS sensors and distributed architectures. I also explore applications in remote laboratory platforms and engineering education.
